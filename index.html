Let's Play Ping-Pong!

Here is a game that will take your input and return to you a series of numbers where:

  • Divisible by 3 returns ping
  • Dividible by 5 returns pong
  • Divisible by 15 returns pingpong

About Ping-Pong:

  • This version is a bit different than table ping-pong
